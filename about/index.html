About

In the aftermath of the 2016 U.S. election, many of us were left with anger, despair, and a question. “What do we do now?”

The Alliance is our answer to that question. We will fight every day with all the energy we can muster. Some days that may be hours, some it may be minutes. Collectively our thousands of minutes makes lifetimes worth of showing that there are still decent human beings in the world, looking out for each other regardless of the color of our skin, our gender, where we were born, or who we choose to love.

We are all allies.
